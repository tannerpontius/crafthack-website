User Experience

To make the benefits of working with Acuity-VCT clear. We created a homepage that takes visitors through the main areas of information they are most likely looking for: what they do (included a demo video front and center), their specific product offerings, who they work with (industries), and client testimonials (social proof).The rest of the site is very similar in our intention to bring value to the visitor by clearly and concisely providing them with the information they are looking for. Especially with B2B businesses, there’s no need for frills. The unique value proposition and brand identity need to be immediately apparent or you’ve lost your potential customers.
