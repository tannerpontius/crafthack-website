Supporting the growth of a rapidly growing Poké cuisine restaurant chain.

Get your poké on!

overview

After the founders were introduced to the Hawaiian staple on a trip to California. The appeal of this fast and casual—while healthy—concept immediately inspired the founders to begin thinking about how they could bring this unique dish to the midwest. With 20+ locations and many more on the way, the Poké Bros. takeover is well underway.

From the inception, Poké Bros were confident the foodies of Columbus would love the poké concept but wanted to provide an inexpensive and accessible product in the larger market. After some intense research and trial and error, the Poké Bros founders developed their own brand of poké bowl flavor they are proud to share and call their own. Modeling similar assembly line casual dining concepts, we developed a unique way to build your “sushi in a bowl” and have fun doing so!

With their rapid growth, Poké Bros. needed a website solution that could not only handle the tens of thousands of visitors they get each month, but create a site design as unique as their brand while getting visitors to take action through our online ordering system.

Simplified ordering

With close to 20 locations and counting Poke Bros. continues to put maximum emphasis on the quality of their food and service. Between the many locations and customization menu options, we created kept it overly simple when it came to the site and content layout. Want to see the menu? It’s front and center and allows you to quickly get a glance at what’s available to you without too much scrolling.

The ordering system we’ve implemented makes it insanely quick and easy to place your order and be on your way to pick it up in minutes. Not sure what location is closest to you? No problemo! The ordering system automatically detects your location and pulls up the locations in order according to proximity to where you are.
